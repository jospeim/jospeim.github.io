About me

I am a research intern in the ACME project, where I study optimization methods for improving freight transportation from economic, environmental, and societal perspectives.

My current research focuses on horizontal collaboration in freight transportation, with particular emphasis on the mechanisms that limit collaboration, such as cost allocation and heterogeneous transport requirements, and on methods to overcome these challenges.
